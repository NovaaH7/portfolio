UI/UX makeover for Neareo, a social network app.

Working on Neareo app was my first profesionnal experience in UI/UX design.
I had the opportunity to complete a 3 months internship at Neareo - a Belgian startup - in Brussels.
Working on their mobile and desktop social network application, i had to make over the design, navigation, and whole screen structure for some pages. A lot of structural changes were made, based on more accurate user stories.

I also worked on the branding of the startup, and on promotionnal flyers.
