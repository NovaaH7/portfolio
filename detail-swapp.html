Swapp, an app to swap contact infos !

Swapp is a mobile app project, carried out within the context of a 'serious game' held by Alteca.
The goal of this application is to replace the paper version of business cards by a digital one : by simply touching two smartphones, all the contact informations of the owners will be exchanged.

I was charged to design the whole navigation of the app and its structure regarding user stories our team defined. I also worked out the branding, and name of the app. The main goal was too keep it as simple and clean as possible, focusing on its primary goals without unnecessary functionnalities.

Sketch was used to create the screens designs.
