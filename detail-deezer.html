UI/UX Design for a listening posts tablet app.

These screens were designed for the Deezer/FNAC listing post application, a mobile app made by Alteca.
This app is used today in tablets working as listening posts in all FNAC stores in France : it allows customers to scan the barcode of any CD in the store, and instantly listen to extracts of it. People can also browse a selection of albums, or search for music directly through the application, in the Deezer database.

In this project, my role was of a pure UI/UX designer; I had to design clean, simple and easily implementable screens from wireframes guides delivered by the Deezer team.

Sketch was used in order to design the screens, Marvel for prototyping, and Zeplin to share them with the developper team.
